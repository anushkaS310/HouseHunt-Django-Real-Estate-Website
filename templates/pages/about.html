About us

HOUSE HUNT is a website that aims to make the process of searching property for renting or buying labour-saving, cost-effective, and time-efficient.
The Real Estate website, HOUSE HUNT, is developed for the purpose of providing a platform where the properties for selling and renting can be listed.
This project is designed for the purpose to reduce the charges the broker spent while renting or buying a house.
Through this website the tenant or buyer can search property on the basis of his requirements without wandering around thus saving his invaluable time.
